Current Projects

Below I will showcase my current PhD projects and try to give a holistic view of my work.

Uniform Benchmark Framework

The goal of this project is to develop a comprehensive benchmark to compare molecular representations across different domains as similarity-based virutal screening, scaffold hopping potentail, clustering and diversity assessments and machine learning tasks.

Genetic Algorithms

My subsequent project will utilize genetic algorithms to optimize ligands in a generative approach to explore the binding pockets of proteins.
